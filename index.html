Pufferfish Nest Builder

Inspired by the real nesting behaviour of pufferfish discovered on the ocean floor of Japan.

How to Play

Move using the Arrow Keys

Swim over shells and sand fragments to collect them

Return to the nest and press Space to deposit materials

Stay close to your nest as the shark cannot enter the safe zone

Nest Complete

Your nest was successful. The female pufferfish has arrived.

Nest Destroyed

The shark was too dangerous. Try again.
